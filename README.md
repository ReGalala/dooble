## What is this?

Dooble is a simple platform for discovering activities around you.
The idea is to make it easy to find things to do, while also giving organizers a way to share and manage their events.

This repo contains a **demo version** of the app that runs entirely in the browser (no backend).

## Important

This is just a demo:

* There is no real database
* Data is stored locally in the app
* Changes won’t be saved permanently

It’s meant to show how the product could work, not be a finished system.
