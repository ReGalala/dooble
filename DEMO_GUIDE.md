## What to explore

### As a visitor

1. Log in as `funny@email.com`
2. Browse the map — click on any marker to see activity details
3. Hit the **Last-minute** button in the filter bar to see time-sensitive spots
4. Find **Dragons' Den Pitch Night** on the map (Lindholmen area) and click it
5. Go to **My Page** and check:
   - **My Tickets → Upcoming**: a pre-booked Liseberg trip
   - **My Tickets → Past**: a few older activities with a "Rate this" button
   - **My Activities**: the Yard Sale activity you posted yourself

### As a provider

1. Log out and log back in as `mail@chalmers.com`
2. You'll land on the provider dashboard
3. Check that **Dragons' Den Pitch Night** is listed there
4. Click into an activity to see how the detail and management view looks

---

## Good to know

This is a static demo — there's no real backend. All data is mocked and stored locally in the browser. Any changes you make won't be saved once you close the tab or clear your browser storage.
